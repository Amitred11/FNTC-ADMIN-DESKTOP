About FiBear Network Technologies Corp.

FiBear Network Technologies Corp. (FNTC) is an IT Company formally established on June 06, 2022 by a group of fifteen talented and skilled entrepreneurs. The company aims to provide fast, affordable, and reliable internet services across the country.

"During the pandemic lockdowns, President & CEO Prince Seath E. Danalo realized the growing demand for reliable and affordable internet, especially in areas where supply was scarce."

Having seen this as a business opportunity, he collaborated with 14 professionals in various IT fields to form FiBear Network Technologies Corp.

Our Mission

To provide high-speed, affordable, and reliable internet connectivity to every Filipino household and business, empowering communities through innovative digital solutions and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To be the country's most trusted and leading internet service provider, bridging digital gaps and fostering progress through technology-driven connectivity.
